About Me

I am a web developer who brings together clean engineering and creative storytelling. I love turning ideas into interfaces that feel intuitive, expressive, and a little bit magical. My approach combines strong full-stack fundamentals, accessibility, and design-minded thinking—always focused on building experiences that work beautifully on any device.

I have a background in art direction, worldbuilding, and visual design, which deeply influences my work. I think a lot about atmosphere, storytelling, and how interfaces feel—not just how they function. Exploring interactive design has strengthened my ability to build organized UI systems and think through user experience from concept to final polish.

Outside of web development, I stay curious and creative. I love rock climbing, snowboarding, spending time with my two dogs, exploring music, and writing. I also have a strong appreciation for tattoo art and graphic design, which shapes how I think about composition and visual style.
